PERFORMANCE ANALYSIS OF T-20 CRICKET

Indian Premier League (IPL) Analysis

The analysis investigates the systematic covariation among various dimensions pertaining to batting and bowling capabilities of T20 cricket using the advanced statistical technique. The real dataset of 100 batsmen and bowlers has been considered from Indian Premier League (IPL) 2015,2016 and 2017 for analysis purpose.The aim was to analyze the performance of players and the replacement for underperforming players. The findings of this study show that batting capability dominates over bowling capability.
